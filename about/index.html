What this tool is for

The course graph is a visual index of prerequisites, departments, and degree planning choices. It is designed to help you see relationships between classes at a glance and to keep your degree builder plan aligned with your major requirements.

Use it to sanity-check prereqs, compare electives, and quickly spot where a course fits in your schedule.

Reading tips

  • Click a course node to view its details and prerequisites.
  • Use the legend filters to focus on a single department.
  • Search by course code to jump directly to a class.

Degree Builder workflow

Start with a preset to populate the eight semesters, then rearrange as needed. The requirements panel updates as you add or remove courses. The export button saves your plan as an Excel file with semester totals and a degree total.

Data sources

Course data and presets are stored in JSON under main/js/data. Requirements are compiled into requirements.json from the CSV scaffolds in the repo root.
